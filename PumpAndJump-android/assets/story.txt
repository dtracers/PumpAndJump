Clef is an olympic athlete, who represents the great nation of Melodonia. 
Melodonia is a nation that is fueled by music to the point where it's a part of nearly every citizen's live's.
For, example, as a part of his civic duty, Clef is a member of the famous Blue Man Group.
In the last olympics Clef won the gold in every single track and field event. 
However; he has been cursed by the mighty rhythmancer, Presto, who lost a bet because of Clef's victories. 
The curse forces Clef to continually run with no control of his legs. 
It also makes the music that he loved so much cause everything to come after him.
Can you help Clef survive Presto's horrific curse?
